My Journey to Cloud Engineering

My passion for cloud technology began when I realized how AWS and cloud computing are revolutionizing the way businesses operate. Coming from a background in Healthcare, Automotive, and Retail. I knew there were transferrable skills that could be transferred. I was fascinated by the scalability, cost-effectiveness, and innovation that cloud platforms offer.

Through intensive self-study, hands-on labs, and real-world projects, I've developed expertise in AWS services including EC2, S3, Lambda, RDS, and CloudFormation. My approach focuses on solving business problems with cost-effective, scalable solutions.

What drives me is the opportunity to help organizations reduce costs, improve performance, and innovate faster through well-architected cloud solutions. Every project I work on aims to deliver measurable business value.
